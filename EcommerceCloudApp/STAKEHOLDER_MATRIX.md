## Key Insights Summary

### From Verna Abdoul Energies Conversation:

1. **Centralization Need**: Multi-location business owners need a unified platform to manage multiple stores from one interface
2. **Ghana-Focused Products**: Strong emphasis on local/Ghanaian products - this aligns with GoMart's Ghana-focused approach
3. **Dashboard Requirement**: Critical need for comprehensive dashboard functionality to monitor operations
4. **Internal vs External Challenges**: Internal management issues are more pressing than external market challenges
5. **Access Control**: Interest in allowing others to access the platform (suggests multi-vendor or marketplace model)
